Miso Soup

This miso soup is full of savory flavors.

Add more dashi to your soup if you want a stronger stock. You can use yellow, white, or red miso paste for this soup â€” yellow miso is sweet and creamy, red miso is stronger and saltier.

  • 4 Cups Water
  • 2 Teaspoons Dashi Granules
  • 3 Tablespoons Miso Paste
  • 1 Package Tofu, sliced
  • 2 Green Onions, sliced diagonally into 1/2 inch pieces
  1. Combine water and dashi granules in a medium sacuepan over medium-high heat, bring to a boil.
  2. Reduce heat to medium and whisk in miso paste
  3. Stir in tofu
  4. Separate the layers of gren onions and add them to the soup
  5. Simmer gently for 2 to 3 minutes before serving.
